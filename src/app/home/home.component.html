Pizza

The highest quality ingredients, sun-ripened tomatoes, the best olive oil, unique Manitoba flour, sea salt, wild yeast - and plenty of time for the dough to mature. We bake the pizzas in the Ferrari of pizza ovens, a customized stone floor oven from Castelli Forni. The toppings are only partially baked - the ingredients are often placed later or just put back in the oven for a short time. As a result, there are no limits to the imagination of the dishes and the consistency and aromas of the toppings are retained. Our pizzas are compositions and unique.
